How To Play

Outside, drag around the campus map to hear buildings, paths, people, fields and playgrounds. Double tap or use Activate Selected to enter buildings or talk to people.

Inside buildings, use room-based exploration. Choose rooms, people and objects from the lists.

The game saves automatically. It also simulates time while you are away.

People have ongoing stories. Conversations come from their lives, memories, worries, friendships, birthdays, roles and current situations.

Sound files should sit beside this HTML file. If a sound file is missing, the game still works without it.
